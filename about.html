About Me

I was born in the hills of Bavaria where at a young age I was amazed by the beauty of the World. My parents were farmers, and we were a poor family.

As I grew up my parents noticed my fascination with drawing the landscape around me, capturing it. On my 10th birthday they surprised me with a camera so I could capture the beauty of the world more easily.

From that moment on I found my calling, I dedicated my life's work to capturing and sharing the beauty of this world we inhabit.
